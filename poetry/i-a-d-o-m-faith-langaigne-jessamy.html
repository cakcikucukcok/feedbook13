I. A. D.O.M is a collection of poems, rhymes, prose and compositions. It is a refection of the authors philosophy. This first anthology is based on the simple truth that words and poetry is a part of everyday living. Poetry celebrates everything taking the simple and the ordinary and making it into the happy and wonderful, the sad and mournful, joys and laughter. In the process it helps us to heal, remember and to share our thoughts with others. This is why I.A.D.O.M in its simple form comes from the heart. The range and selection of work, is a celebration of the different emotions that touch our lives as it connects us with one another.
