Have you ever wondered what would happen if Santas team of reindeer got sick just before their big flight? Is there a way to help the reindeer stay healthy on Christmas Eve? In this colorful, fun-filled rhyming book, children and adults alike will learn of the Christmas that was saved by Eddie the Elf and Doc. Doc and Eddie create a mysterious concoction to heal the reindeer of a sudden illness that strikes just a few days before Christmas. Eddie the Elfs hidden talent is the key to creating The Treat after Doc cannot find a cure of his own. With no time to train new reindeer to take flight on Christmas Eve, Santa needs a healthy crew. Find out how you can ensure Santa will successfully finish his flight with a team full of strong reindeer. Make sure to make a batch of The Treat to leave for Santas reindeer on Christmas Eve. A recipe for the reindeer snack is included and is guaranteed to save the reindeer from illness this Christmas.
