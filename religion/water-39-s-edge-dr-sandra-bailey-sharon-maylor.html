Through biblical stories, personal reflections, and practical tips, Waters Edge challenges the reader to take a step into a God direction in order to live more authentically and maintain inner peace. In each days reading you will discover meditations bathed in scripture that will help bring clarity to the difficult decisions you are faced with in everyday life.

Perfect for individual devotions or as a basis for prayer partners and group studies, Waters Edge: A 31-Day Devotional invites the reader to leave the mundane shores of complacency and soak in the promises of God. Each days devotional ends with one of the following: a prayer, reflection, steps for change, or plan of action that you can apply to your circumstances. Take the plunge. Set aside time to reflect, recharge, and refresh. Step into the waters of possibilities. Wade out a little bit deeper into the sea of biblical promises and know that God is by your side every step of the way.
