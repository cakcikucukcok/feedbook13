Thought the snow and the woods of Lapland the cunning fox moves around misleading the smartest hunter and the strongest bear.

Four unforgettable Lapland’s tales, a unique journey with northernmost’s animals.

The tricks of the fox is a tales’ collection for kids and teens, the stories in this volume are The tricks of the fox, The jackdaw and the fox, The ptarmigan and jackdaw, Sarakka and the animals.

The tales are from the book Lappiske eventyr og folkensagn - Lapland’s tales and folklore- and they are also a useful reference for the scholars of Lapland’s culture.
