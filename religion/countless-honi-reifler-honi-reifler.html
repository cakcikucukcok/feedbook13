A collection of anecdotes reflecting on some of the countless miracles of God at work through the ministry of Sherwood Christian Rehab Centre, Glenreagh, NSW, Australia, published for the 40 year anniversary in January 2018. Honi Reifler, who with her husband JP pioneered Sherwood Christian Rehab Centre, recounts some of the miracles they have seen and experienced during the 40 years of the ministry of Sherwood. By leaning totally on God to provide in every situation, they have seen God provide in remarkable situations, and seen God's power at work in people lives in exraordinary ways. These stories are not only encouragement but proof that God is active in people's lives today.
