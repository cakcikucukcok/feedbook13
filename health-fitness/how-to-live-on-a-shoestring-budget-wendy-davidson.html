With the downturn in the economy and so many job cutbacks, many are struggling to make ends meet. They suffer frustration and do not know where to turn. This book offers proven strategies for cutting costs, living on less, and how to live practically. These tips are proven methods to help singles and families live with less without going into massive debt. This book is a small investment that offers great dividends if the methods and strategies are put into practice. Written in a concise, lighthearted, and easy to follow format, "How to Live On a Shoestring Budget" is a great tool for all who need some help to manage their budget in any economy, but more so in today's leaner times. It also makes a great gift for a family member of friend facing some economic challenges.
