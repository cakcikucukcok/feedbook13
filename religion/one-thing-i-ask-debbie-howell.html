One Thing I Ask is an inviting, refreshing, encouraging, inspirational book for any Christian who is looking to develop, grow deeper in, or re-ignite their relationship with God through His Word. This how-to book filled with powerful scriptures throughout begins each chapter with the authors riveting personal testimony of her Christian journey & real-life experiences and ends with teachings on relevant & practical ways to incorporate and make Gods Word a personal part of ones everyday life. This book is perfect for any Christian who hungers to know God more intimately & is searching for a more fulfilling relationship with God than ever before!
