Grandpa Garnier used to say, Love is like a bucking bronco.

It takes guts and determination to hang on, but it's worth the ride. If only Grandpa was around now to help Tyson Garnier out of this mess. The famous pro football player is suddenly saddled with a very public scandal–and an illegitimate child. Tyson needs a good nanny now if he's going to salvage his career.

And plain, no-nonsense Dakota Brown is the ideal candidate. Dakota doesn't care for football–or for a man who doesn't seem to want his own son. But she needs this job, and that precious little baby needs her. Dakota isn't Tyson's kind of woman. But the more he sees of her–and the more he sees of his son–the harder he wants to hang on.
