‘’If love steals life, you have no choice," said an old man sitting on the corner of a street. I noticed these words when an old man whispered.

The old man seemed strangely affected. By nature, he looked like an old trace of a forbidden family. His long, scattered hair showed the lack of thoughtful hands around him, and his covering material revealed the extinction of the people around him.

I approached him and apologized to my friends who were joking and laughing. I went to see the elderly person and asked; "Around the world, human development has changed? I don't want to be disrespectful, but I am revealing the truth and it is my art and my profession.

" The old man was not touched by my words and continued to mumble. over and over again, but maybe he was lost.

Then I put my hand on his shoulder. He looked up and looked deep into my eyes, then lowered his head. I was surprised by this strange behaviour, but I asked with curiosity: "Oh, old man, speak! ‘’Because the force in you cannot resist silence."
