Friendship bracelets never go out of style. And with this fun friendship bracelet ebook, they're easier and more fun than ever!

Ultimate Friendship Bracelets shows you everything you need to make 12 classic braceletsskeins of floss,, beads to add if you're in the mood, and a book of clear step-by-step instructions and diagrams. PLUS, downloadable video demos makes each of these projects super-easy.

This ebook features all the classics, from 2-color chevrons, stripes and diamonds to funky 6-color totems, plus a heart pattern, a tasseled a zigzag, and more. And there are some great ideas for embellishing your bracelets to make them even more personal.

There's still nothing more meaningful to give someone special than a friendship bracelet, made by you. So grab this ebook and have fun making that little gift that says "from me, to youwith love."
