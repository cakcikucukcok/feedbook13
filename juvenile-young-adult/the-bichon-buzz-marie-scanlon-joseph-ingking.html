I started jumping up and down, wagging my tail, and barking as if to say, Please take me with you! The Bichon Buzz is a lively story about an adorable puppy who desperately tries to capture the attention and warm the hearts of two special visitors at the pet store. What is this clever puppy up to? How will she grab the attention of this little boy and girl? She has a few moves under her paws, one which includes peeing on their shoes! Children, get ready to laugh! This six-pound, white, and fluffy Bichon Frise will grab your attention and warm your hearts too! Does she get adopted and finally get a family to call her own? Its a game of chase for you and the puppy as you read to discover the answer.
