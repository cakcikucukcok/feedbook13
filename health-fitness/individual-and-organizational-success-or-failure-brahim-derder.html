Do you want to be successful or are you scared of success? Are you perfect? Do you know anyone who is perfect? Do you think you can improve and be happier, more successful, and a better person? Did you ever wonder why some people keep doing the same things (usually mistakes) that keep them failures even though success can be theirs if they only acted differently? This book shows you how to be successful and embrace it with courage. Life is a simple system: you were born, raised, and when you reached school age you attended classes and learned a system of reading and writing, and perhaps you majored in some field. It is up to you not your teachers, or bosses, or your parents, or anyone else to act and make your life a success and get you what you want from it, because nobody else will hand it to you. You can do it. Just start today and right now; good things will happen for people who try. Good things will happen for you.
