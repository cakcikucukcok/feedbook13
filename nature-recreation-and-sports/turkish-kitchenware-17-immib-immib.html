We greet you once again with an Ambiente Fair special issue. In 2015 IMMIB (Istanbul

Minerals and Metals Exporter’s Associations) takes the scene at the 67th instance of the

annual Ambiente fair, in which Turkey has participated on a national scale for the 20th

time, in halls 3.0, 5.0, and 10.1, and with 67 firms. As you know, the Ambiente fair is the

prestigious far for firms operating in the house and kitchen wares, and souvenir sectors.

Aside from individual participants, Turkish firms which offer a wide product range

including metal kitchenware, small electrical appliances, plastic kitchen and house

wares, glass items, yard furniture and gift items draw the attention of purchasers with

their competitive prices and superior quality products.

We would like to impart a few brief information points regarding the fairs in which we

had participated before. 46 Turkish firms in total attended the Paris Packaging Fair,

held in Paris with the participation of 1500 firms and 500 brands worldwide. Over

55 percent of the fair’s participants come from outside France, and the fair receives

over 100 thousand visitors from 100 countries. We also took our place at the Zuchex

Indonesia Fair held in Jakarta, Indonesia, in which the Turkish national organization was

implemented for the first time by IMMIB. We have implemented out seventh overseas

marketing event for Toronto-Montreal, Canada, that had been determined as a target

market within the strategy formed for the Turkish House and Kitchen Ware Sector

Overseas Marketing Team.
