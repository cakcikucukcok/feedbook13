This is a guide to cultural engagement for success, designed for leaders and managers of all levels in every industry. The cultural engagement handbook combines elements of culture strategy, culture models, culture assessment, culture planning, and initiatives to transform the culture to an engaged and high-performing one. This handbook provides guidance through a simple seven-step approach called SUCCESS: Step One - Set the Culture Strategy; Step Two - Understand the Current Culture; Step Three - Consider the Culture Gaps; Step Four - Create the Culture Plan; Step Five - Execute the Culture Plan; Step Six - Sustain Cultural Engagement; Step Seven - Salute Cultural Engagement. Employing this seven-step approach results in successful development and execution of a culture strategy, culminating in an engaged, high-performing culture. With the purchase of the cultural engagement handbook, the reader has access to a downloadable file containing all templates referenced in the handbook.
