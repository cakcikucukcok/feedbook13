The Old Testament prophet Jeremiah was called by God in his young life to a daunting and pivotal task. Nevertheless, God prepared him for ministry in the first nineteen verses of his book. In this forty-day journey, author John A. Coaxum dissects Jeremiah chapter one verse by verse to reveal what the calling of God really is and is not.

Many dash into ministry hastily and end up disillusioned and disappointed because of expectations God never promised and sacrifices they were never ready to make. This book is a practical guide for anyone who senses God calling them to serve and what they should know before they answer.
