This is the age of self-expression, thanks to the multiplicity of social media. Self-publishing is what I would refer to as the high end of social media where persons like myself can express our thoughts with meaning and freedom. We dont have to wait on the high gods of the publishing world to give us the rights of publication. I wanted to step outside of my world of political science and publish my thoughts on a variety of topics, most of which have no relation to politics. I wanted to share my thoughts with a wider community of readers and so I decided to share my long-held beliefs about self-improvement, society, love, forgiveness and a host of other topics. Some will be readily accepted, perhaps, while others will cause some to wonder if they can buy that idea. Yet, other ideas may not be accepted as good ideas at all. Thats okay. One of the goals of the book is to stimulate thought, and develop an ongoing conversation on thoughts Ive expressed in the book. Please read and ponder the meaning of each thought and feel free to share your own thoughts with me about any that triggers interest or disagreement. Im still on the learning curve of life and can learn from my readers as well. I can be reached at kcsimms@gmail.com. Each email will be given full attention in my answer.
