In the fourth book in a series, Nadia Mei, a six-foot-tall white panda, travels throughout China

with her leopard companion, Claw, searching for Dream Land.

As the story begin, Nadia Mei and Claw encounter poachers who shoot Nadia Mei in the leg

when she uses her body to shield her friend. Wounded and bleeding, she is captured and placed

inside a cage on a truck.

When the truck becomes submerged in a river, Selah the owl sees Nadia Mei and offers

assistance by unlocking the cage door and releasing the wounded panda.

After attending to the pandas injuries, Selah fl ies back to the river searching for other animals.

She finds Claw, and eventually all three embark on the journey together.
