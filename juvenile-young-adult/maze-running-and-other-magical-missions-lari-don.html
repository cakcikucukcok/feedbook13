When Yann the centaur is stabbed through the heart by a unicorn horn, the life-threatening injury needs a magical remedy. The friends split into teams: Lee, Helen and Sapphire search for King Arthur's scabbard; Catesby, Lavender and Sylvie search for hidden gems in a cliff face; Rona and Tangaroa wash a flower in seven waterfalls at sunrise. This is the fourth and final book in Lari Don's popular First Aid for Fairies series.
